ABOUT ME

Hi there, I am Joy, a 3rd year student from Republic Polytechnic, currently pursuing a Diploma in Digital Design & Development. In school, I am often active in my CCA (SOI Club), participating in the various service learning and workshops held. On top of that, I also serve in the Girls in Tech @ Cross Polytechnic group as one of the EXCOs for publicity. During my free time, I enjoy reading books on politics, watching Hong Kong dramas and immersing myself into topics pertaining to cybersecurity. Outside of school, I serve with the Girls' Brigade as an officer and in my church youth ministry as part of the media crew.
